The Foundation

CIFO: Cisneros Fontanals Art Foundation

Established in 2002, CIFO has become a vital platform for Latin American artists, providing grants, commissions, and exhibitions that have reached audiences from Miami to Madrid, São Paulo to Zurich.

Our mission extends beyond collecting—we're building bridges between cultures, supporting emerging artists, and ensuring that Latin American voices are heard on the global stage.

"Art has the power to transcend borders and unite humanity. Through CIFO, we're not just preserving art—we're cultivating understanding and fostering dialogue across cultures."

— Ella Fontanals-Cisneros

The Journey

From Cuba to the World

Born in Cuba, raised in Venezuela, and now a global advocate for Latin American art. My journey began with teaching water ballet and evolved into founding one of the most significant platforms for contemporary Latin American artists.

Through CIFO and my personal collection, I've worked to ensure that the voices of Latin American artists resonate in museums, galleries, and hearts worldwide—from the Metropolitan Museum of Art to the Tate Modern.
